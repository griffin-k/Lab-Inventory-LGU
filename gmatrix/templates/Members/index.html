About Us

Mission

Garrison Engineering and Robotics Society (GEARS) is dedicated to fostering a comprehensive understanding of robotics among aspiring engineers. Through engaging workshops and competitive events, we aim to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills essential for excelling in robotics technology. By laying a strong foundation, we empower our members to become leaders in the field.

Vision

At GEARS, our vision is to cultivate a community of skilled individuals passionate about robotics and automation. We strive to provide students with hands-on experience, enabling them to achieve self-sufficiency and innovation in robotics technology. By nurturing creativity and technical expertise, we aim to inspire the next generation of robotics leaders.
