Mission

Garrison Engineering and Robotics Society (GEARS) strives to acquaint aspiring engineers with both the theoretical and practical aspects of robotics technology through workshops and competitions. These events lay the foundation for students to excel in the field of robotics.

Vision

Our aim is to nurture and empower students in the field of robotics and automation, providing them with hands-on experience to become self-sufficient.

Our Activities

  • Participation in national and international robotics competitions.
  • Line Navigation
  • Indigenous Robotics
  • Modular Robotics
  • Robot SUMO
  • Robot Football
  • Training students for international-level robotics competitions.
  • Organizing robotics competitions and workshops.

Our Aim

Our core aim at GEARS is to promote excellence in robotics and engineering. We emphasize the values of teamwork, innovation, and continuous learning.
